Who is using?

Most industries that work with large amounts of data have recognized the value of machine learning technology. By extracting insights from this data – often in real time – organizations are able to work more efficiently or gain a competitive advantage over their competitors.

Financial services

Most industries that work with large amounts of data have recognized the value of machine learning technology. By extracting insights from this data – often in real time – organizations are able to work more efficiently or gain a competitive advantage over their competitors.

Government

Government agencies such as public safety and utilities have a particular need for machine learning as they have multiple data sources that can be mined to gain insights from them. Analyzing sensor data, for example, identifies ways to increase efficiency and save money. Machine learning can also help detect fraud and minimize identity theft.

Healthcare providers

Machine learning is a growing trend in healthcare thanks to the advent of wearable devices and sensors that allow healthcare professionals to access patient data in real time. The technology can also help medical specialists analyze data to identify trends or alerts, leading to improved diagnoses and treatments.

Marketing and sales

Websites that recommend products and services based on your past purchases are using machine learning to analyze your purchase history – and promote other items you might be interested in. This ability to capture data, analyze it, and use it to personalize the shopping experience (or implement a marketing campaign) is the future of retail.

Oil and Gas

Discover new energy sources. Analyze minerals in the soil. Predict refinery sensor failures. Accelerate oil distribution to make it more efficient and cost-effective. The number of machine learning applications in this industry is vast – and growing.

Transport

Analyzing data to identify patterns and trends is essential for the transportation industry, which depends on designing more efficient routes and anticipating potential problems to increase profitability. The data modeling and analysis aspects of machine learning are important tools for carriers, public transport and other industry organizations.
